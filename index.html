About Rollaxe Flooring & Home

With over 25 years of experience, we provide complete and professional home renovation services for residential and commercial customers. Our services include flooring (carpet, hardwood, laminate, vinyl plank/tile, finished custom carpentry, custom stairs refinishing), kitchen renovations, painting & drywall, home service repairs, and more. Our services are provided for homes and businesses in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).
